# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, constructive, and kind.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully.
- Help keep discussions useful and on topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Public or private intimidation.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Reporting

If you need to report a concern, open a private security/contact channel when available or contact the maintainer through GitHub.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who do not follow this code of conduct.
